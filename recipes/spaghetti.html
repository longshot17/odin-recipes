Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description:

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a simple, yet flavorful Italian pasta dish made with just a few key ingredients: spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, red pepper flakes, and fresh parsley. The garlic is gently sautéed in olive oil until golden, releasing its rich aroma. Red pepper flakes add a hint of spice, while the pasta is tossed in the infused oil for a silky, savory finish. A sprinkle of fresh parsley adds a touch of brightness. This quick, satisfying dish highlights the beauty of minimalist cooking, with a perfect balance of heat, garlic, and olive oil.

Recipe:

Ingredients:

• 400g (14oz) spaghetti

• 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced

• 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

• 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)

• Salt (for pasta water and seasoning)

• Freshly ground black pepper (optional)

• Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

• Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.

• While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.

• Add the sliced garlic to the pan and sauté gently until golden brown and fragrant (about 2-3 minutes), being careful not to burn the garlic.

• Add the red pepper flakes to the pan and stir for another 30 seconds.

• Reserve about 1 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain the spaghetti.

• Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the garlic oil, tossing to coat evenly. Add some reserved pasta water to achieve a silky sauce.

• Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste.

• Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve immediately. Enjoy!
