Avocado Toast

Description:

Avocado toast is a simple, yet satisfying dish featuring ripe avocado spread over toasted bread. The creamy avocado is often seasoned with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil, with optional toppings like a squeeze of lemon, red pepper flakes, or a poached egg. The combination of crispy toast and smooth, flavorful avocado makes for a nutritious and delicious meal or snack. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or anytime you need a quick, wholesome bite.

Recipe:

Ingredients:

• 1 ripe avocado

• 2 slices of your choice of bread (sourdough, whole grain, etc.)

• Salt, to taste

• Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

• Olive oil (optional)

• 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)

• Red pepper flakes (optional)

• 1 poached egg (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

• Toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispness.

• While the bread toasts, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

• Mash the avocado with a fork, adding salt, pepper, and lemon juice (if using) to taste.

• Once the bread is toasted, spread the mashed avocado evenly over each slice.

• Drizzle with a little olive oil (optional) for extra richness.

• Sprinkle with red pepper flakes for a bit of heat, if desired.

• Top with a poached egg for added protein (optional).

• Serve immediately and enjoy!
